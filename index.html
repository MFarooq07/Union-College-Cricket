About US

Cricket club is not just a club, its home away from home to many! Club members
are not just students, they are a family.

Official Practices

Club members gather weekly for practices over weekends to in a match like environment to work on their skills.

Personal Private Practices

Practices of 2 to 4 individuals solely meant to work on specific weakness/strength

Watch Parties for games

Not playing a game for any reason? No worries! Come and cheer for us even on away games with free transportation!

Watch parties for cricket matches

Big fan of watching cricket tournaments? We got you! Year-round screening of crucial ICC games.

Lift days (Optional)

Better fitness better game! Join your peers in Alumni Gym to train for the cricket games and practices.

Post game food

Discuss the highs and lows of a game in a post game discussion on lunch/dinner table and have fun!
